Privacy & Data Use

Plain language: We collect only what you choose to send (name, email, your message). We use it to reply or update you—nothing more. We don’t sell, rent, or share your data.

What we collect

  • Join form: name, email, region, short reflection, consent preference.
  • Contact form: name, email, message.

How we use it

  • To respond to your message or onboarding request.
  • To send research updates if you consented.

Retention & control

  • You can ask us to delete your data anytime: tom@openpeople.me.
  • We minimise retention and periodically purge old submissions.

Telemetry

We use privacy-first, cookieless analytics to understand page usage—no cross-site tracking, no personal profiles.

Updates

We’ll update this page as the protocol evolves. Last updated: October 2025.
