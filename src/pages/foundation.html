Foundation

Stewarding the protocol.

The Open People Foundation is the non-profit guardianship layer for the protocol — neutral, transparent, and accountable to the public.

We protect the mission, publish the roadmap, and maintain safeguards that keep the protocol aligned with people, not platforms.

Focus

  • Ethical governance & bylaws
  • Open research & documentation
  • Protocol funding & grants
  • Community onboarding & care
Stewardship

Mission first

The Foundation maintains the charter, the alignment tests, and the non-profit requirements that keep Open People neutral and censorship resistant.

Transparency

Open disclosure

Budgets, decisions, and proposals are published openly. Every major action includes a rationale, a recorded vote, and a public feedback window.

Participation

Community oversight

Members can submit proposals, flag drift, and request audits. The Foundation’s job is to make participation easy and meaningful.

Governance stack

Open People operates on transparent, auditable processes. The Foundation executes within a layered system so power stays distributed.

  • Board cadence: quarterly public meetings + emergency sessions for protocol risk.
  • Ethics council: rotating members review AI alignment, consent, and data use.
  • Community assembly: open contributors respond to proposals before ratification.
  • Protocol guardians: maintainers empowered to pause rollouts if safeguards fail.

2025 Priorities

  1. Publish the full governance charter and bylaws.
  2. Launch the public grant program for protocol-aligned pilots.
  3. Release transparency reports on funding, audits, and onboarding.

Open funding

All resources are tracked through public ledgers. Funding sources and uses are documented in quarterly releases. Grants require measurable alignment tests.

We prioritise funding for experiments that prove the protocol works in lived environments — cultural sites, cooperatives, and other human systems.

Funding channels

  • Mission-aligned donors with transparency covenants.
  • On-chain treasury for protocol maintenance.
  • Grants for open research & accessibility.

Get involved

Support spans more than funding. We welcome contributors who can help develop policy, design audits, run pilots, or care for community onboarding.
