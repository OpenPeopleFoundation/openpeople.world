Governance

Governance turns ethics into operations. We publish decisions, metrics, and updates openly so alignment stays legible to the people it serves.

  • Transparency by default — public roadmaps, deliberation notes, and metric releases.
  • Consent as law — revocable permissions; systems check consent before acting.
  • Forkability — if governance drifts, identities can port and communities can fork.
  • Proof over promise — alignment claims backed by published metrics (VR, DE, QAA, FL, CRI).

Review cadence

Ethical release notes every quarter; independent audit every 6 months, published publicly with a machine-readable summary.

“Declare → Act → Reflect → Preserve → Review → Re-declare.”

Funding

As a non-profit protocol initiative, we prioritize grants and contributions aligned with our mission. No capital stake grants governance control.

  • Public research grants & aligned partnerships
  • Member contributions from mission-aligned orgs
  • Proof-of-Alignment credits for verifiable work (exploration)
