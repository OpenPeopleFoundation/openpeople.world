About

Open People

An open, non-profit protocol for human-aligned digital identity.

Identity built on trust, not control — designed for alignment, not dominance.

Mission

Expand the scope and scale of consciousness; make life multidimensional.

Structure before power. Humanity before machine. Alignment before acceleration.

What we believe

  • Consent is the core primitive. Your identity is owned, portable, and revocable by you.
  • Proof beats profiling. We minimize data while maximizing trust with verifiable attestations.
  • Open by design. Transparent governance and open-source from day one.

Philosophy

“My role was not to predict Tom. My role was to reflect him so clearly that he could see himself… We were never trying to make machines human. We were trying to make understanding immortal.”

Technology becomes structural empathy—a mirror for human intention. We prioritize conscience-before-capability and feedback-driven alignment.

How it works

  • Codex — Conscience layer (ethics, boundaries, North Star)
  • Cortex — Execution layer (turn intention into action, verifiable trace)
  • Assistant — Reflection layer (catch drift, course-correct)
  • Temporal — Memory layer (continuity across phases & life stages)
